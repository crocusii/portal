Nope. - Esolang
Not to be confused with Nope!, nor nope.

Nope. is the constant language ConstantLanguage("Nope."). You can code anything you would like, but the language will neither compile nor process it. This unfortunately means that Nope. is not Turing-complete. You could still create a quine and a cat program[see discussion], and infinite number of self interpreters.
